About Me

Hello! I'm Rajendra Paudyal, a PhD student in Computer Science at George Mason University. My research focuses on Next Generation Networks, Wireless Communication and Network Security, where I'm working to make intelligent, smart and robust network.

Prior to my doctoral studies, I completed my Master's degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Tribhuvan University.

Research Interests

  • Next Generation Netwoks (5G/6G)
  • Application of AI/ML in Wireless network
  • Computer Network
  • Network Security

Currently, I'm working on a project that will help on efficient utilization of frequency spectrum. This research has potential applications in 5G communication (5G/6G), and I'm excited about its possibilities to improve on 5G footprint, faster deployment, and network performance with scalability.
